A knowledge graph is a structured representation of facts and relationships between entities.

Nodes (Entities): People, places, objects, concepts.
Edges (Relationships): How entities are connected (e.g., "Paris is the capital of France").

Why use Knowledge Graphs?
Improve search (Google Knowledge Graph).
Enable reasoning & inference.
